/** Commands for creating gemset **/

1 . rvm version-name   (rvm 1.9.3)
2 . rvm gemset create gemsetname  (rvm gemset create gemset)
3 . rvm use version-name@gemsetname (rvm use 1.9.3@gemset)
4 . rvm install gem        (rvm install simple_form)
5 . ls `rvm gemdir`/gems   --- command to list gems in gemset
